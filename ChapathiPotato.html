CHAPATHI & POTATO CURRY

Estimated Calorie Intake : 1641 calories

Health Benefits :
  • Rich in Fiber
  • Good Source of Energy
  • Nutrient-Rich
  • High in Potassium
  • Antioxidant Properties

How to prepare :
  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add diced potatoes and mix to coat them with the spices.
  7. Add water to cover the potatoes and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender.
  8. Add garam masala and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
