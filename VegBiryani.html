How to prepare :
  1. Cook Rice: Parboil basmati rice until it's 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare Vegetables: Saut√© whole spices in ghee or oil, add sliced onions, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and cook until onions are golden brown. Add chopped tomatoes and mixed vegetables, cook until partially done.
  3. Layering: In a pot, layer the vegetable mixture and parboiled rice. Sprinkle garam masala, fried onions, and saffron milk between layers.
  4. Cook: Cover the pot and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Serve: Fluff the biryani, garnish with fried onions and fresh coriander leaves, and serve hot.
