DOSA

Estimated Calorie Intake : 167.67 calories

Health Benefits :
  • Simple to Digest
  • Abundant in Carbohydrates
  • Excellent Protein source

How to prepare :
  1. Soak Ingredients: Soak 1 cup of rice and 1/2 cup of urad dal (black gram) separately in water for about 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind: Drain the water and grind the rice and urad dal separately into a smooth batter. Mix them together and add a pinch of salt.
  3. Ferment: Let the batter ferment overnight or for about 8-12 hours in a warm place.
  4. Prepare Dosa: Heat a non-stick pan or griddle. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa. Drizzle a little oil around the edges.
  5. Cook: Cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy. Fold it and serve hot with chutney and sambar.
